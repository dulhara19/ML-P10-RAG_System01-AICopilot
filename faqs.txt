Q: How to apply for leave?
A: Visit the student portal > Leave section. Fill out the online form and submit. Approval takes 2 days.

Q: How to request transcripts?
A: Go to the Exams Branch site. Click “Request Transcript” and complete the form. Processing takes 7 working days.

Q: What are library hours?
A: Monday to Friday, 8AM to 6PM. Closed on weekends and public holidays.

Q: How do I reset my student email password?
A: Visit the IT Helpdesk with your student ID or email support@university.edu.
